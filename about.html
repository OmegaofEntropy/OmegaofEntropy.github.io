Biography

My name is Amy Smith, and I have been working to establish a career in science since the age of seven. At fifteen years old I began attending college in Pennsylvania in addition to my usual workload of highschool credits. This marked the official beginning of my journey that so far has led to a doctorate in geology and vertebrate paleontology. I have had the privelege of working at and graduating from universities in New York, Michigan, and Virginia, which has given me an appreciation of the wide variety of needs, viewpoints, and methods within education, outreach, and science. I have also managed four different natural history collections at four different institutions, and am looking to move on to a full time job in a related field.

Goals of This Website

  • to promote and help to facilitate inquiry based, student centered learning within various topics of science

  • to support the educational and outreach efforts of universities, museums, and other institutions

  • to provide educational resources and opportunities to a diverse audience ranging from academia to the everyday person (and everyone in between) in hopes of encouraging an inquisitive fascination in science

  • to support the aquisition and maintenance of intellectual control of museum collections through contributions to collections stewardship

  • to provide musuem advocacy and faciliate community engagement
