Zines

A zine is an 8 page mini booklet that is printed on a piece of 8.5 by 11 inch paper. After printing, this paper is then cut and folded in a certain procedure that renders it a pocket-sized magazine. Zines are a concise way to explore a variety of topics. Please feel free to peruse the following growing library of zines. All zines are free for download, use, and distribution provided that:

  • The author's name and all information remains visible on the work.
  • When applicable, the name(s) and/or information of the organization for which the material was created remains visible on the work.

Zine Assembly Instructions: Click on the title page of the zine to open its PDF file. When printing, please make sure that the the "Page Scaling" option under "Page Handling" in the pop-up print box is set to "None". Next, for assembly of the zine, please follow the instuctions presented in this pdf (pdf provided by COPUS).
