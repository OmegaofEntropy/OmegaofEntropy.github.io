Object Removal Cards

One of the easiest ways for an object to become lost is through inadequate documentation of the object's whereabouts once it leaves its home location. This page offers object replacement cards that can be downloaded, printed, and copied to minimize potential object loss. Specific removal cards have been made for instances when objects are on display within the institution versus when objects are on loan as a way to further document the object's history.

The first row of object removal cards are without museum logos and may be used by any institution. The following rows of cards are labelled with logos of institutions, which are indicated to the left. All cards are 3 inches across by 2 inches tall, and print 10 to a sheet. They are free for download, use, and distribution provided that:

  • The author's name and all information remains visible on the work.
  • When applicable, the name(s) and/or information of the organization for which the material was created remains visible on the work.

Printing Instructions:Please click on the object removal card image to open its PDF file. When printing, please make sure that the "Actual Size" option under "Size Options" in the pop-up print box is selected.
