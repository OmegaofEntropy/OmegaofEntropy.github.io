Activities

The goal of these activities is to promote inquiry-based, student centered learning through hands-on exercises. They are available for use by schools, homeschoolers, universities, museums, or anyone with an interest in science! All activities are free for download, use, and distribution, provided that:

  • The author's name and all information remains visible on the work.
  • When applicable, the name(s) and/or information of the organization for which the material was created remains visible on the work.

Activities below are categorized into various scientific topics, and list the necessary supplies, targeted grade levels, and pertinent keywords. If you are looking to support a specific Standard of Learning, please use the Science Standards of Learning Menu to find which resources support your SOL in question.

Please click on the activity title to open the PDF in a new window.
