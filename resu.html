OBJECTIVE

To fulfill the institution’s mission statement by performing collections stewardship and curation, including establishing and maintaining intellectual control of the collection, facilitating collection access, using specimens in research and outreach efforts, promoting professional development, contributing to institutional publications, collaborating with local and statewide entities, and acquiring outside funds for support.
