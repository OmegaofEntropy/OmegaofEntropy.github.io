Specimen Labels

Here I offer a growing collection of various labels useful for natural history collections. The labels are contained in *.pdf format, and would be best printed onto 8.5"x11" acid-free paper. All labels are free for download, use, and distribution provided that:

  • The author's name and all information remains visible on the original printout (before cutting out labels).
  • When applicable, the name(s) and/or information of the organization for which the material was created remains visible on the work.

Please click the label image to download the *.pdf.


2.75" wide x 1.5" tall

Blank Arsenic-Bearing Specimen Labels

These blank labels can succinctly alert anyone perusing the collection to the presence of arsenic within certain specimens, and further emphasize the importance of proper specimen handling.

Blank Radioactive Specimen Labels

These blank labels can alert anyone perusing the collection to the presence of radioactive specimens, and further emphasize the importance of proper specimen handling.
