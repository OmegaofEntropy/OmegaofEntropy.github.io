Museum Audiences and Science Standards of Learning

Educational and Outreach efforts are unproductive if the proper audiences are not addressed. I see museums as having three major categories of audiences: the general public, people afilliated with grades from prekindergarten through twelve, and members of higher Academia. These main categories can be split up into the following subcategories:

Science Standards of Learning, or SOLs, are one of the ways to easily include the pK-12 audiences within outreach efforts. By integrating National and State SOLs into activities, exhibits, kits, literatures, workshops, and other endeavours, public and private education can be supported by materials and efforts that already reach other audiences. This site links to the National Science Standards of Learning as well as several different State Standards of Learning, which I hope to increase regularly. The educational materials offered on this site are also cross-referenced with these SOLs. To navigate the SOL pages, please see the options below the Audiences & SOLs tab above.
