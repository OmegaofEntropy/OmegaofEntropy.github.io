Educational Materials

The Blue Pteranodon offers educational materials and activities to museums, universities and schools, educators, and students. These materials fall under various science categories, often within biology, geology, and paleontology, among others. For those looking to support specific Science Standards of learning, these resources are also cross referenced unter the appropriate SOL section. All materials are 100% free for download, use, and distribution as long as the following conditions are met:

  • The author's name and all information remains visible on the work.
  • When applicable, the name(s) and/or information of the organization for which the material was created remains visible on the work.

Select the major category of educational materials from the menu above, or peruse the annotated list below:

Zines

 A zine is an 8.5" by 11" piece of paper folded up into a collectible, tradable, 8 page book. Zines are a succinct way to supply information about a certain topic.

Activities

 These activities can be performed in a classroom or labroom setting, at a museum during outreach activities, and even in a more informal setting with the proper supplies and supervision. All activities have their targeted grade ranges indicated.
